Our History

Founded in 1990, our school has been a cornerstone of academic excellence for over three decades. What started as a small institution has grown into a comprehensive educational center that serves hundreds of students annually.

Throughout our history, we have maintained our commitment to providing quality education while adapting to modern educational needs and technological advancements.
